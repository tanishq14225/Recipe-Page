## 🖥️ Preview
**Ingredients:**  
- 1 cup flour  
- 1/2 cup sugar  
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- 1/2 cup milk  
- 1/4 cup butter  
- 1 egg  

**Instructions:**  
1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).  
2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.  
3. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.  
4. Pour into a greased baking pan.  
5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until done.  
6. Let cool, slice, and enjoy!
